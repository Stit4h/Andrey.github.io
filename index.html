"Lilo and Stitch"

"Lilo & Stitch" - 42th full-lengt animated Walt Disney Studios film, which was released in 2002. On June 21, it was released in theaters in North America. The premiere of the version, dubbed in Russian, took place in cinemas since December 26, 2002 in all countries of the Commonwealth of Independent States.

Main characters:

  1. Stitch
  2. Lilo Pelekai
  3. Jamba Jukiba
  4. Agent Wendy Pleakley
  5. Nani Pelekai
  6. David Cowina
  7. Cobra Bubbles

Stitch is an alien genetic experiment created by Galaxy Defense Industries engineer Jamba Juquiba. The main character of the cartoon. Experiment 626 is designed to create chaos and destruction. The genetic experiment is distinguished by exceptional irascibility and destructive behavior. Lilo was re-educated into a loyal and good friend, always ready to help. Stitch's favorite drink is coffee, the book is "The Ugly Duckling", films (at first) - destructive, about healthy monsters and spiders. Loves rock and roll and coconut cakes.

Lilo Pelekai is the main character of the cartoon. Little girl 7 years old (at the beginning of the film 6 years old), living in the Hawaiian Islands on the island of Kauai with her older sister Nani. A very extraordinary and eccentric personality, which causes difficulties in her communication with peers. Therefore, Lilo makes up for this deficiency in communicating with Stitch, whom others take for a dog. Lilo attends hula and one of her hobbies is taking pictures of people on the beach. In addition, Lilo loves horror movies, sci-fi movies. But at the same time, she is a very kind person who, with her kindness, turns a space monster into a sweet and affectionate creature. Lilo's idol is Elvis Presley. The girl loves to draw and feed the Puzan fish with a jam sandwich.

Jamba Jukiba is an alien from the planet Quiltacuan. He worked as an engineer at Galaxy Defense Industries on the planet Turo. Initially he wanted to catch Stitch, but in the end he helps Lilo rescue and becomes a good friend of Lilo and Nani, and a member of the family. However, he is prone to lying, often makes rash acts and quickly changes his mind. Jamba is brilliantly intelligent and developed in almost all areas of science. Despite the fact that he considers himself an evil genius, he is very kind. He was sent to Earth along with Agent Pleakley to capture Experiment 626. Jamba is a member of the club of space evil geniuses, specializing in genetic mutants.

Agent Wendy Pleakley is an Other Planet Observer and Earth Expert. He was sent to Earth in order to look after Jamba and help him in capturing Stitch, but in the end he took part in saving Lilo and became a good friend to Lilo and Nani, and a member of the family. Pleakley has three legs, a swivel neck, one huge eye, two tongues, and a small head. Hysterical, whiny, prone to panic and fainting. Likes to wear dresses and wigs, posing as an earthly woman. Afraid of his own family and marriage. He has a mania to put things in order, yell, interfere in everything and be the first to run away. Very self-confident. Jamba loves to scare him and watch him scream and run away. Pleakley is cowardly and very talkative. Fashion specialist.

Nani Pelekai is Lilo's older sister. He has been taking care of Lilo since their parents died, is very afraid of losing her and loves her very much.

David Cowina is a surfer and fakir friend of Lilo and Nani. Tries to ask Nani out on a date.

Cobra Bubbles is a former CIA agent who saved the planet from aliens, now a social worker. Cobra Bubbles is ubiquitous. He quickly learns about everything and often appears at the most inconvenient time. He is familiar with the head of the Galactic Federation Council.
